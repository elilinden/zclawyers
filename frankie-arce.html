Frankie Arce

Intake Specialist

About Frankie Arce

Frankie Arce is an Intake Specialist at Zaghi & Chrzan, LLP, where she serves as a primary point of contact for prospective clients and plays a key role in evaluating and developing new cases. She brings a strong background in legal support, insurance claims management, and client relations, with experience across personal injury, insurance, and litigation environments.

Intake & Case Development

In her role, Frankie conducts detailed client intakes, gathers and assesses critical information, and ensures that each matter is efficiently developed from the outset. She is known for her ability to communicate clearly with clients, maintain organized case documentation, and ensure timely follow-ups in fast-paced legal settings.

Professional Background

Prior to joining the firm, Frankie gained extensive experience handling client intake, managing insurance claims, drafting legal correspondence, and coordinating communication between clients, medical providers, and legal teams. She has also supported litigation efforts by preparing demand packages, organizing medical records and billing documentation, and assisting with case development from intake through resolution. Her background includes significant experience in document collection and tracking, ensuring accuracy and completeness of key case materials.

Client Service & Skills

In addition to her legal experience, Frankie has a strong foundation in customer service, where she developed advanced communication, problem-solving, and client management skills. She is bilingual in English and Spanish and is proficient in a wide range of legal and case management platforms, including Clio, Filevine, Salesforce Litify, Google Workspace, and Microsoft Office.

Frankie brings a client-focused, detail-oriented approach to her work and is committed to providing responsive, organized, and professional support to every individual who contacts the firm.
