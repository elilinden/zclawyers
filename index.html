Questions We Hear Often

Frequently Asked Questions

Straightforward answers to the questions California workers most often ask before starting a claim.

How much does it cost to speak with an employment lawyer?
Consultations are free and confidential, and the firm handles cases on a contingency basis. That means you pay no legal fees unless the case is successful.
What kinds of workplace cases do you handle?
We help with wrongful termination, discrimination, retaliation, wage claims, harassment, leave violations, accommodation claims, and class actions.
How quickly should I talk to a lawyer after a workplace issue?
As soon as possible. Employment claims have strict deadlines, and early guidance can help preserve documents, witness information, and the strongest timeline for your case.
Where can I learn more before I contact you?
Start with the employment law blog or read practical guides like what to do after a wrongful termination and how to document discrimination.
