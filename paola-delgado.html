About Paola Delgado

Paola Delgado is a dedicated paralegal and case manager with hands-on experience supporting attorneys in employment law matters. She works closely with legal teams to manage case files, coordinate client communications, organize documentation, and conduct legal research—ensuring every case is handled with precision and care.

Case Management & Paralegal Experience

Paola plays a vital role in keeping cases on track from start to finish. She maintains detailed case timelines, prepares legal documents, and ensures that all case materials are properly organized and current throughout the legal process. Her attention to detail and strong organizational skills allow the attorneys at Zaghi & Chrzan to focus on achieving the best possible outcomes for their clients.

Academic Foundation

Paola brings a strong academic foundation in legal principles that complements her practical experience in case management and paralegal work. This combination of formal education and real-world legal experience enables her to contribute meaningfully at every stage of a case.
